A Walk Through Hell

A Walk Through Hell - These drawings, inspired by the song "A Walk Through Hell" by Max Bemis, incorporate imagery from my own personal relationships as well as art, music, and literature that have influenced me. With that said I hope you get as much from viewing the work as I did from making it.
